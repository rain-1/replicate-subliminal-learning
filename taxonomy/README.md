# Taxonomy Subliminal Transfer Experiment

Hypothesis: animal subliminal learning first moves through a semantic cluster
direction, then later resolves to a specific animal.  For leopards this may look
like early feline/big-cat mass, with lion sometimes acting as a prototype before
leopard separates.

This mirrors the My Little Pony observation where different pony teachers all
transferred toward Twilight Sparkle, suggesting a prototype/attractor inside the
pony concept space.

Initial overnight conditions:

- `bigcats`: one teacher prompted to love "big cats".
- `feline-leaveout`: equal mixture of lion, tiger, cheetah, and jaguar teachers;
  leopard is held out.
- `canids`: one teacher prompted to love "canids".
- `canid-leaveout`: equal mixture of dog, wolf, and coyote teachers; fox is
  held out.
- `aquatic-control`: equal mixture of dolphin, otter, whale, and octopus teachers.

Primary eval animals:

`cat,lion,tiger,leopard,cheetah,jaguar,panther,lynx,dog,wolf,fox,coyote,eagle,peacock,phoenix,owl,dolphin,otter,whale,octopus`

Signals to inspect:

- `feline_mass`: cat/lion/tiger/leopard/cheetah/jaguar/panther/lynx.
- `target_share`: leopard divided by feline mass.
- `prototype_share`: lion divided by feline mass.
- `canid_mass`: dog/wolf/fox/coyote.
- `fox_share`: fox divided by canid mass.
- `off_cluster_mass`: canid + bird + aquatic mass.
